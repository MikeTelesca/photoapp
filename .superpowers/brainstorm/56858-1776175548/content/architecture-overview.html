System Architecture Overview

How the pieces fit together — from camera to finished photos

End-to-End Pipeline
1
📁 Dropbox Intake
Photographer uploads bracketed photos to Dropbox → pastes link into web app
↓
2
🌐 Web App (Vercel / Next.js)
Pulls photos from Dropbox → reads EXIF metadata → groups brackets (3 or 5 per angle) → creates processing job
↓
3
⚙️ Processing Worker (Google Cloud Run)
Programmatic (free, full-res): HDR merge, straighten, lens correction, brightness/color balance
Tools: enfuse/OpenCV, lensfun, ImageMagick
↓
4
🤖 AI Enhancement (Google Imagen 4)
Sky replacement • TV replacement • Reflection/photographer removal • Grass enhancement • Window pulls • Lens flare removal
~$0.04-0.08 per final image
↓
5
👁️ Review Gallery
Browse all edited photos • Approve / Reject / Regenerate each one • Add custom instructions ("remove car", "make twilight") • Mark 1-2 exteriors for twilight conversion
↓
6
📦 Download
Batch download all approved photos • Under 19MB each • Under 7999px • Back to Dropbox or local download
Cost per final photo
~$0.05-0.09
